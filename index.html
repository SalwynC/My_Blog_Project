Kyoto: A Hidden Gem of Japan

Discover Kyoto's serene beauty, from its ancient temples to the bustling streets of Gion. A guide for those who want to experience Japan's soul...

Read More

Known for its breathtaking temples and tranquil gardens, Kyoto is one of Japan's most historic and picturesque cities. Whether you’re exploring ancient shrines or sipping matcha in a cozy tea house, Kyoto is sure to leave a lasting impression...

Top Spots in Kyoto You Can’t Miss

  • Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion): A must-see Zen Buddhist temple covered in gold leaf.
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine: Famous for its thousands of red torii gates winding through the forest.
  • Gion District: Wander through this traditional geisha district at dusk for a truly immersive experience.
